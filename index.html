Our Story: Food Without Fear


Our Goal and Misson

Our company started with a seemingly simple goal: make dining a worry-free experience through allergy conscious catering.

Our Story

Our founder, Priya Sharma, faced this challenge for years, always feeling alienated when it came time to celebrate life's biggest moments with food.


Rather than feeling a sense of togethernees, Priya had to skip family meals, worry about accidental exposure to allergens, and feel alone during events that should've been full of joy.


Growing up, she missed out on many things that kids her age would normally enjoy due to her nut and gluten allergies.


Priya recognized the importance of creating an inclusive meal environment and made it her life's mission to ensure that no one with allergies would feel excluded in social settings.


What began as a one woman operation in Priya's small Chicago apartment has now blossomed into an allergy friendly catering empire!
